## My Role
- Compliance, Operational, Regulatory and Governance Lead
- Broad remit covering team operations, team development, governance, regulatory
  reporting, and external stakeholder engagement
- No direct reports
- Regular context switching across multiple workstreams is a core feature of my role
- **Career Level: L6** (Regulatory, Programs & Advisory track)

### What L6 Means in Practice (from the Compliance Career Ladder)
- Owns major programmes or initiatives and serves as an expert resource across multiple teams
- Interprets complex regulations, leads risk assessments, and designs scalable processes or controls
- Shapes strategy within their domain, contributes to decision-making, and leads cross-functional alignment
- Communicates recommendations and risks with clarity across levels, including senior stakeholders
- Influences outcomes through trusted relationships and mentors others
- Operates with a high degree of autonomy in solving complex, ambiguous problems that span processes, tools, or products
- Key competencies at L6:
  - **Risk Expertise:** Designs risk management approaches and leads reviews spanning multiple functions; evaluates control strategy gaps and balances mitigation with operational tradeoffs
  - **Regulatory Expertise:** Leads regulatory strategy within a domain; translates complex/emerging regulations into scalable operational approaches; coaches others in regulatory interpretation
  - **Problem Solving:** Designs decision frameworks and leads resolution of cross-functional compliance challenges; coaches others in analytical reasoning and stakeholder-aligned decision-making
  - **Leads Through Change:** Leads cross-functional change initiatives; anticipates concerns, sequences change intentionally, and builds team-wide resilience
  - **Knowledge Sharing:** Builds and maintains scalable knowledge systems; ensures regulatory/policy updates are reflected in team practices
  - **Operational Excellence:** Leads improvements across systems and domains; influences how compliance is operationalised through tooling, process, or policy
  - **Applies Expertise:** Recognised as a technical leader across teams; applies knowledge to high-impact, ambiguous situations and helps define standards and risk tolerances
